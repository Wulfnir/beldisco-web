Request permanent deletion of your BELDISCO account and data

If you would like to delete your BELDISCO account, you can send us a deletion request by email. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving your request.

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile (username, display name, avatar, bio)
  • All diary entries, ratings, and reviews
  • Your followers and following relationships
  • Your likes and comments
  • Your notification history

Please note that deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Request Deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your BELDISCO account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
