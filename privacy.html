Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

BELDISCO ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use BELDISCO, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, username, and display name
  • Profile information: avatar image, bio, and other profile details you choose to provide
  • Diary entries: albums you log, ratings, reviews, and listening dates
  • Social data: who you follow, likes, and comments
  • Push notification token: a device token used to send you notifications (only if you grant permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the BELDISCO service
  • Display your profile and diary to other users
  • Send notifications about activity on your account (follows, likes, comments)
  • Improve the app and user experience

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

BELDISCO uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend infrastructure. Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Deezer API — used to fetch album and artist metadata. We do not share your personal data with Deezer.
  • Expo — used to deliver push notifications to your device. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us, which will result in the permanent deletion of your profile and all associated data.

5. Children's Privacy

BELDISCO is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via the app settings

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage and secure authentication. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of BELDISCO after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at:
privacy@beldisco.com
